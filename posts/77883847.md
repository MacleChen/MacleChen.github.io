![](images/posts/c49fa0b6febc.webp)

3、把iconfont.css和iconfont.tff文件分别拷贝到项目文件css和fonts下。打开iconfont.css文件如下：

![](images/posts/43496e102558.webp)

![](images/posts/a0c98295aa6e.webp)

<link href="css/iconfont.css" rel="stylesheet"/>
